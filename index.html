ABOUT ME

My name is Iraida Serrano-Martinez, though I just go by E.D. I’m a bi-lingual techie and a collector of random knowledge. Though most recently I have seen that since the future is being paved with technology, I should move with it.
I find that there is little difference between the every changing world of technology and nerd culture. I find myself in an interesting position everyday. A place where I can put my skills to practical use and be out in the world rather then sitting behind a computer all day in a dark basement. By sharing what I know about computers and other tech with others, I can make technology seem just a little less intimidating. I keep up to date with the newest ideas and systems in the computer world because I love seeing how technology is paving the way to the future. I also love finding creative way to share what I know even if it means dressing up as Robin or the Joker. Here, let me explain.

E.D.


I’m a cosplayer, which stands for costume play. I get to dress up as my favorite characters or ideas from movies, TV shows or books. I use this to promote literacy for kids occasionally. I dress up and read comics to kids and adults because what better way to make reading less scary then having a superhero show you its fun. I have also taught kids some computer basics while dressed up. There's something about having Robin teach you how to login into websites that makes it more captivating.
As a collector of random knowledge, I seem to have amassed a few certifications along the way. I’m CompTIA A+ certified, ServSafe certified, and have basic First Aid & CPR training certification. I’m also studying for my CompTIA Network+ exam, so yeah I like staying busy.
